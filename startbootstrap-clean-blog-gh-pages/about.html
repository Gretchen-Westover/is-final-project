About Me

This is who I am and what I stand for.

I am a passionate BYU business student planning to major in Strategic Management and Spanish. My aspirations include: conducting international business in the Spanish language and working as an internal strategist for Apple or Amazon.

I have included a page about Barcelona because I spent a year and a half living there as a full-time missionary for my church. I fell in love with the people, culture, language, and beautiful landscapes of Barcelona. I plan to return as often as time and money permit.

In my spare time I can be found hiking, discovering news songs, playing violin, running, or watching a beach sunset. I am one of 3 siblings and one of my main goals in life is to become a loving wife and mom.
